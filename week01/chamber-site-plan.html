Chamber Site Plan

MDC Chamber of Commerce

MDC = Municipalidad del Distrito Central

Site Purpose

The MDC Chamber of Commerce acts as a municipality organization where enterprenurship leaders can establish solid foundations on municipality and international economic principles through the constant learning oportunities provided by local and foreign trusted institutions.

Scenarios

1. When are the future training events occur?

2. What are the requirements to join the chamber?

3. What are the benefits and services provided by the chamber?

Color Schema

White: FDFFFC, Blue: rgb(0, 0, 149), Green: 629460, Yellow: F1D302, Silver: C9C7C7

The blue, white and silver color will be used as a background, the yellow will be used to decorate some headings going as a underline, on top of that, blue, white and black colors will be used as font colors, varying on the sections of the page where they more fit in, the idea is to represent the formal image of the chamber.

Typography

Family: Libre Baskerville for headings. Family: Playfair Display for most paragraph text.
